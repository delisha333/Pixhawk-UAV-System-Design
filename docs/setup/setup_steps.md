# Setup and Assembly Process

This section describes the actual steps followed during drone assembly.

## Step 1: Frame assembly
The quadcopter frame was assembled and checked for structural stability.

## Step 2: Motor mounting
BLDC motors were mounted on each arm with proper alignment.

## Step 3: ESC connection
Each motor was connected to an ESC, and ESCs were connected to the KK controller outputs.

## Step 4: Power system
LiPo battery was connected to power the ESCs and controller.

## Step 5: Controller mounting
KK 2.1.5 controller was mounted at the center of the frame.

Care was taken to keep it level.

## Step 6: ESC calibration
All ESCs were calibrated to ensure uniform motor response.

## Step 7: Motor testing
Motor rotation direction was verified and corrected if needed.

## Step 8: Parameter tuning
Basic tuning was performed using the onboard LCD interface.

---

## Important note

Proper setup is critical.  
Even small mistakes in wiring or calibration can lead to unstable flight.
